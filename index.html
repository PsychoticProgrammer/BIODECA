Biodeca is a venture that seeks to help the planet by manufacturing products that are environmentally friendly. We have made two products, a pencil made with reused newspaper that has a second use and a plastic case which degrades after 20 days of being in contact with water.

We started with the idea of creating ecological products because the pollution of our planet today is very serious; besides killing innocent beings that have no control over our actions. The neglect of the planet by the human being has been increasing and it seems that people do not care about it. That is why we came up; to encourage people to make a change and start helping the planet. This is just the beginning and an incentive for people to become aware and we hope that they will also change their way ofthinking.

OBJECTIVES

Our goal is to reduce the excessive felling of trees for the manufacture of pencils; it is known that this has become a problem since 15.3 billion trees are currently felled for their production. With our pencils, in addition to reusing newsprint, we help reduce habitat destruction and biodiversity loss.

The pencil has a capsule with seeds on the top; we put this in so that when the pencil is no longer useful you can plant it in the ground and make a plant grow. And so also if our pencils start to be used en masse, there will be many more plants.

In addition, with our biodegradable sleeves we aim to save millions of marine lives, as 100,000 marine animals are killed by plastics every year. Turtles mistake plastic bags for jellyfish and seabirds have their stomachs filled with plastic items. This debris also affects the health of reefs.

The plastic bags are non-toxic, so when ingested by animals they do not cause harm, and it is estimated that the amount of plastic bags found in the sea ranges from 5 to 50 billion fragments, not including the pieces that are on the seabed or on the beaches. Therefore, by dissolving in the water, it solves one of the biggest environmental problems present on earth today.

Making
To make it we use newspaper, which we roll it around a 2mm graphite lead with a unique paste prepared in a special way that allows a better adhesion to the lead. On the top we put a capsule with plant seeds for its second use and let it dry for one or two days. And that's it! We have an ecological pencil.
Manufacture of the cover
Mix 30g of cornstarch, 198ml of water, 19.8ml of white vinegar and 19.8ml of glycerin in a pot and boil it until it has a thick consistency. Once in this state, it is poured on a square aluminum sheet. Let it dry for 48 hours and give it the desired shape. And that's it! We have a non-toxic biodegradable cover.
